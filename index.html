About AlgoPlayground

AlgoPlayground was created with a simple mission: make algorithm learning accessible, interactive, and fun. Traditional algorithm education often relies heavily on mathematical notation and abstract concepts that can be difficult to grasp.


We believe that algorithms are best understood through direct interaction and visualization. By providing interactive simulations, we help learners build intuition for complex concepts through exploration and play.


Whether you're a student, educator, or professional looking to deepen your understanding of algorithms, AlgoPlayground offers a hands-on approach that complements traditional learning resources.
